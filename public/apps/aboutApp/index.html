What is this?

You may have noticed, however, that what you see here is not the same as the IRIX workstation in the Original Jurassic Park movie.

The reason for this is the answer to the following question: do you have any idea what IRIX is?

The anser is no

Even if Silicon Graphics only became defunct in as late as 2009, not many people have any idea what the company was, and not many people have ever used the actual operating system. It is thus not very impressive for anyone.

We, on the other hand, wanted to make something that would be impressive, and, well, Silicon Graphics just didn't really achieve that goal. Most people, however, have used a Mac before, and have seen the MacOS operating system.

This is MacOS Rhapsody

At about the same as IRIX was being developped, Apple was also creating an operating system of its own, which greatly ressembled IRIX, called Mac OS X Server 1.0.

Before releasing Mac OS X Server 1.0, however, Apple released unstable versions only meant for developers. These were called Rhapsody Developer Release 1 and Rhapsody Developer Release 2, respectively.

The robotics website is based off of Rhapsody Developer Release 2. Although We have attempted to emulate most of its features, it is not indetical

Using the Site

Installing Apps

Each app on the operating system represents a website component as per the CRC rulebook. Since this is a new account, there is no app currently installed on the system.

Originally, you would have had to use some kind of browser to obtain apps, just as we do today. Here, however, installing apps is done through the App Installer. Open the App Installer by pressing on the Tools menu at the top of your screen and clicking App Installer.

Then, in the menu that opens, select the app you wish to install and click Install.

Opening Apps

Once the app is installed, you can head over to the desktop (keep in mind that the windows can be dragged by grabbing their top) and double-click on the icon.

Uninstalling Apps

To delete an app, you must simply click on the App Uninstaller under Tools and click Uninstall.

Changing the Language

To change the language of the operating system, head over to Tools and click on Preferences.

Then, click on Language and Open

Then, click on your desired language and click Select
