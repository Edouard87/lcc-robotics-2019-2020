What is this?

Yes, the Lower Canada College team is centered around the theme of Jurassic Park, but the website team thought simply making a regular website would be quite boaring––not to mention the fact that it could be accompplished using builders like Word Press

We wanted to make something that would truly fascinare the judges. As we were thinking about the possibilities of the website, we came accross the following scene from teh movie.

Here, the main character logs into an old-looking computer and secures the entire building. That was the springboard for the entire website.

Indeed, this website is themed around the Rhapsody operating system, a typical operating system for the time that greatly ressembles the one you saw on screen in the movie.

This operating system, however, is dissimilar than the one you saw in the movie, which was an operating system called IRIX developed by the Silicon Graphics Co. This operating system is a mockup of the Apple Rhapsody operating system.

The sole explanation for the discrepancy is that we, the robotics team, found the IRIX operating system relatively boaring for most. On the other hand, most people have used a mac at some point in their lives, and thus, seeing its ancestor may be a more exhilerating experience.
