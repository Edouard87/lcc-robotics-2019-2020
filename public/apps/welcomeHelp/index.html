This is the LCC Robotics Website for the Flip competition

Yes, the Lower Canada College team is centered around the theme of Jurassic Park, but the website team thought simply making a regular website would be quite boaring––not to mention the fact that it could be constructed using regular website builders such as Word Press!

We wanted to make something that would truly fascinare you, the users.

The inspiration for the website comes from the following scene, from the original Jurassic Park movie.

Please note that the below video does not belong to Lower Canada College and has been uploaded by a third party who is not affiliated with Lower Canada College.

"It's a unix system, I know this!"

Here, the main character logs into an IRIX workstation (made by the Silicon Graphics Corporation) and secures the entire building using a very old app called File System Viewer, saving them the group from the dinausaurs

IRIX

Images of the operating system used in the film

IRIX was an extremely advanced operating system for it's time. It was meant to be used for complex graphic rendering.

Please note that none of these images belong to or are affiliated with Lower Canada College.

Today, because of the incredible developpement of technology. IRIX would most likely be viewed as an extyremely archaic piece of equipement. Your computer is much more powerful than any IRIX worsktation.

We wanted to theme the website off of this operating system.
